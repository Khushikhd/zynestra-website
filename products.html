About Gengies

Gengies is a mobile-first social exploration app to discover local spots with verified companions — safe, social, and memorable.

Key Features
  • Verified users & OTP check-ins
  • SOS & emergency helpers
  • Ratings & reviews for safe meetups
Mission

To make city exploration safe and exciting when shared with others.
