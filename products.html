About Gengies

Gengies is a city exploration and experience-sharing app designed to make outings more fun, safe, and memorable. Whether you’re at home or traveling, Gengies helps you discover cafés, restaurants, parks, events, and hidden gems — all with the unique touch of exploring alongside someone new.

Key Features
  • Verified explorers & trusted venues
  • Secure OTP check-ins for safety
  • Ratings & reviews for transparency
  • Temporary in-app chat during outings
  • SOS emergency button with live location
  • Report system for quick issue resolution
Mission

To make city life less lonely, more adventurous, and safe by connecting people through shared experiences. We aim to build the largest explorer community in India, redefining exploration as not just about places, but the people you share them with.
